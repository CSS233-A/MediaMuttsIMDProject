Getting Started: Setting Up Your Web Space

Activate Web Publishing

Necessary components:

UW NetID

You must have a UW NetId (e.g. be a student of the University of Washington). If you already have an YourUserName@u.washington.edu email address, then you already have a UW NetID and you can proceed to the next step. If you don't have one, then you need to obtain one here.

Activate Necessary Services

There are two services that you must activate on the computing services page.
  1. Go to the UW NetID website, found here and log on.
  2. The Student Web Publishing service:

    On the "Computing Services" page, you should see "Student Web Publishing" under the list of services that are turned on. If the service "Student Web Publishing" is not active, then you must activate it:

    1. Find it in the list of services that are inactive
    2. Check the checkbox beside it
    3. Click the Subscribe button and follow the remaining instructions
  3. The Dante Account service:

    On the "Computing Services" page, you should see "Dante Account" in the list of services turned on. If the service "Dante Account" is not turned on, then you must activate it:

    1. Find it in the list of services that are inactive
    2. Check the checkbox beside it
    3. Click the Subscribe button and follow the remaining instructions
  4. Congratulations! You have successfully set up your very own student web space. Proceed to Getting Started: Your Web Page to continue with the Getting Started Tutorials.

    5. Activating Student Web Publishing and the Dante services creates your Web directory called public_html on your Dante account. Files placed into public_html will be immediately viewable on your Web site.
