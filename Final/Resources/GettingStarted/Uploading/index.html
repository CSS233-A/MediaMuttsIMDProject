Getting Started: Uploading Your Files to Your Web Space

Upload Your Web Page to Your Web Space

  1. Open up the FTP client program of your choice, or if you do not have one, visit the Required Programs page and download the appropriate one for your operating system.
  2. Enter the following information in the specified fields and connect to the server:
    • Hostname/Server: dante.u.washington.edu
    • Username: Your UW NetID Username
    • Password: Your UW NetID Password
    • Port: 22
  3. Once the server connection has been established, navigate your Desktop directory and select the index.html file you created. Drag the file to the public_html folder.
  4. Congratulations! You now have your very own home page. To view it, go to http://students.washington.edu/YourUWNetID/. Success! You have completed the Getting Started Tutorials. You can now create and upload files to your web space.
