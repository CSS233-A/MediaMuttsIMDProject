Getting Started

Setting Up Your Web Space

The University of Washington gives students the opportunity to create their own website that is hosted by the UW servers. UW gives students 5 GB of drive space for their website. This service is completely free to students who have a UW NetID.

Creating Your Web Page

Creating your web page is a very easy task. All you need is a text editing program (NOT a word processing program), less than 10 lines of HTML, and a few minutes of your time. HTML is a very easy to learn markup language, even for complete beginners.

Uploading Your Web Page

Uploading files via the File Transfer Protocol (FTP) is a very simple process when one uses a FTP Client program. With FTP, file transferring is quickened, saving much time and energy for the web developer. Many FTP programs replicate the functionalities of operating systems.
